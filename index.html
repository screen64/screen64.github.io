How do I use Screen64?

Capture

Use your set keyboard shortcuts to open a resizable dialog to capture you screenshot.

Base64ify or Upload

You choose if you would like to get a base64 encoded, web browser compatible string or to upload your image to our servers and get a short url.

Share

Share your screenshot with friends, family or anyone. Most forums support both base64 encoded images and images on the internet.

Features:

Convert screenshot to base64.

The base64 sting is browser compatible and works in most forums and in the HTML img tag.

Upload screenshot to the internet.

Allows you to create a short url that links to your image. Works with all forums that support adding images.

Convert any file to a base64 string.

You have the option to create a HTML webpage that links to a download to the file. All of this works offline and does not use the internet.



Whats the price?

Screen64 is free and will forever be free.
The servers we use to host online images is hosted by Google so you know they will always be online.
